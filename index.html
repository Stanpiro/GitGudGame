How to Play

This game works like an iddle clicker

◽Get Strong = 1 XP Point

◽More Muscles = 1 Strength Point, costs 10 XP

◽More Smart = 1 Intelect Point, costs 10 XP

◽Better Weapon = Adds 1 Points to Weapon, costs 10 Gold

◽Better Armor = Adds 1 Point to Armor, costs 10 Gold

◽More HP = Adds 4 Points to HP, costs 5 XP

◽More Mana = Adds 2 Points to Mana, costs 5 XP

◽Hire PT = 1 Point to P.T., costs 50 XP

◽HP = Your Hit Points, 0 or lower means you have died

◽Mana = Your actual Mana to spend

◽Strength = Adds 1 point to your Damage and 1 more point to More HP

◽Intelect = Adds 3 point to your Spelldmg and 1 more point to More Mana

◽Weapon = Adds 3 points to your Damage

◽Armor = Reduce 1 point to Enemy Damage

◽P.T. = Adds 1 point to Get Strong

Game Over
